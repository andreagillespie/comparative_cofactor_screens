# comparative_cofactor_screens
Scripts for analysis conducted in revisions of Bell, et al manuscript: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-024-01749-z
Experiments inclulded in this repo are:

ATACseq_221111: ATACseq performed in EWS, GR, IRF1, MYB, NFKB, NOTCH, p53, PU-1, & VP64 lines treated with DMSO & BRG1.

ChIP_230912: Chips for INTS5, MED12, MED14, & MED25 treated w/ DMSO, CDK8i, dTAG, TNFa & dTAG-TNFa.

CUTandTAG_221109: CUT&TAG performed in EWS, GR, IRF1, MYB, NFKB, NOTCH, p53, PU-1, & VP64 lines w/ antibodies for K27ac & K4me3.
